Aziz Sancar, recipient of the Nobel prize in Chemistry in 2015,
is a Turkish-American biochemist and molecular biologist specializing in DNA repair,
the circadian clock and cell cycle checkpoints. Not one to forget his roots,
Sancar has co-founded the Aziz and Gwen Sancar Foundation,
a non-profit organization,
dedicated to upholding the Turkish culture and sustaining
the Turkish students in the United States.
